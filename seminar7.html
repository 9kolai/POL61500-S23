Digital Democratic Innovations (or Civic tech)

Is there a special "type" of digital democratic innovations?

Or a special type of digital technology for democratic innovation?

Gilman & Peixoto's definition of "civic tech":

"A technology that is explicitly designed and leveraged to increase and deepen democratic participation."

Democratic goods of digital technologies in democratic innovations?

Main take-aways from this week

  • Digital technologies can be used in many different ways in the design of democratic innovaitons.
  • There are both potentials and challenges related to this.
  • There are several challenges in studying digital technologies in relation to democratic innovations:
    1. How to isolate the effect of the "digital", since digital technologies often are combined with institutional changes.
    2. How do we define or characterise the "digital" element? Digital technologies can take many forms.
